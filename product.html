The Craftsmanship

Every Kavish Saree is a testament to the skill of the Devanga community of weavers. This specific piece features the 'Thazhampoo' (Screw Pine) border motif, traditionally symbolizing purity and grace. The cotton is mercerized for a silk-like sheen while maintaining the breathable comfort of cotton.
