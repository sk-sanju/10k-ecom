About Us

Welcome to Kerala Traditional Clothing Store, your destination for authentic Kerala traditional wear sourced from the renowned weaving village of Kuthampully.

Kuthampully is famous for its rich handloom heritage and traditional craftsmanship that has been preserved for generations. We proudly bring these timeless designs to customers across Kerala and beyond through our online platform.

Our collection includes traditional Kerala sarees, kasavu mundu, set mundu, ethnic wear, and handloom products crafted with attention to quality, comfort, and cultural authenticity.

We aim to support local artisans while making traditional Kerala fashion accessible to everyone through a simple and convenient shopping experience.
