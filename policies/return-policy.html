Return & Exchange Policy

Because each of our garments is painstakingly hand-spun and hand-loomed by master weavers in Kuthampully, we take immense pride in our quality. We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase.

1. Custom & Handloom Nature

Minor irregularities in weaving, color shades, or threads are characteristic features of manual pit-loom weaving and are not considered defects. They add to the unique heritage charm of the hand-crafted saree.

2. Return Eligibility

Returns or exchanges are accepted within 7 days of delivery only in the case of major physical damage or receiving an incorrect product. Items must be unused, unwashed, and in their original packaging with the GI tags intact.

3. Exchange Process

To request an exchange or return, please capture a photo/video of the issue and contact our WhatsApp Support team at +91 98470 12345. We will coordinate reverse pick-up and process your replacement immediately.
