Shipping & Delivery

We deliver Kuthampully's sovereign handloom creations to consumers globally. Our packaging ensures your garments arrive pristine, clean, and ready for your celebrations.

1. Processing Timelines

In-stock items are packaged and dispatched from our Kuthampully workshop within 24 to 48 business hours. For custom-made murals or specialized sizing, weaving and coloring may take 7 to 14 days before dispatch.

2. Domestic Shipping

We offer FREE shipping across India. Standard domestic deliveries take 3 to 6 business days depending on your pin code.

3. International Shipping

We ship worldwide. International orders are handled by premium carriers (DHL/FedEx). Shipping costs are calculated during WhatsApp checkout, and international delivery takes 5 to 10 business days.
