Privacy Policy

At Kuthampully Heritage, we value the trust you place in us. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you visit or make inquiries on our store.

1. Information We Collect

When you browse our store, we automatically collect certain device info (IP, browser type, cookies). When you fill out our contact or order forms, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and any details you provide.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to process your inquiries, arrange shipping, coordinate payments, and communicate order details directly with you via WhatsApp or Email.

3. WhatsApp Ordering Checkout

Our ordering flow initiates a direct WhatsApp message from your device to our workshop. The order content is processed directly through WhatsApp's encrypted platform in compliance with their terms of service.

4. Security

We implement industry-standard physical and digital measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, loss, or theft.
